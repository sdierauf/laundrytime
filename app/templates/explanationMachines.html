Washing Modes

Sometimes is so complicated understand how work some washing machine! Imagine the explanation in some language that you don't understand. Impossible, frustrating..! But don't worry we can help you! You will just have to looking for your type machine and you can start to wash your clothes!

Washing Machine Types

This machine is the basic and typical machine. First of all you should introduce your laundry inside, after that you can insert the soap (liquid soap or soap powerds both of them work correctly) in the left box. When you finish this process, you should to choose which program do you want with turning the right wheel. There are some different options. Depending on your type of clothes that you introduce in the machine you should choose the program (if you need help with the different programs you can see on the bottom of this page). When you had chosen your program you can add some characteristics like speed, more water, change the temperature or time.
This machine is a little bit different than the first machine. First of all you should introduce your laundry inside, after that you can insert the soap (liquid soap or soap powerds both of them work correctly) in the left box. When you finish this process, you should to choose which program do you want but in this machine you can choose the program with the buttons not with a wheel. There are some different options. Depending on your type of clothes that you introduce in the machine you should choose the program (if you need help with the different programs you can see on the bottom of this page). In this machine you choose all of the characterustics with the buttons, temperature, time, ...

Programs Types

+ The dark clothes should be wash with cold water because it can fade. Also the jeans and trousers are better to wash with cold water for keep the real color.
+ The white or light clothes can be wash with hot water, but be careful not only depends with the color! The weave is important because the bool or some cotton clothes can't be wash with hot water because the clothes be shrink.
+ The towels and bed sheets should wash with a hot water not less than 60 because you should to disinfect correctly this types of clothes that you use everyday. The sports clothes too.
+ The shits and other special clothes should be wash with a special program, like handwashing program or some other program.
