About us

LaundryTime, a website of more than 1 million students can use the laundry service of their residence.

Authors

Daniel Carballal, Stefan Dierauf, Carla Diví and Germán García are exchange students of Kungliga Tekniska Högskolan (KTH) and this website is a project from one course for Master of Computer Science: Interaction Programming and Dynamic Web. This project is an opportunity to practice the basics of well-architected GUI programming by practicing the Model-View-Controller paradigm on a larger task that includes real data and persistence. Also for practice interactive software development in a group and reflect on the experience.
